This extension provides state-of-the-art protections, designed for maximum privacy and usability. Works out of the box: zero configuration required!

KEY PROTECTIONS

• Block Ads & Trackers: Stops privacy-invading ads, tracking scripts and pixels from being loaded in your browser.
• Advanced Fingerprinting Resistance: Spoofs 18+ system and hardware signals (Canvas, WebGL, AudioContext, Fonts, Time Zone and more) that could otherwise be used to identify you.
• Breadcrumb Removal: Stops tracking parameters, minimizes referrer headers, and stops cache leaks.
• Harden Browser Privacy: Blocks third-party cookies and the Topics API, and enforces the Global Privacy Control.

STRICT PRIVACY GUARANTEE

• 100% Open Source: Our code is open for everyone to examine and verify.
• Zero Data Collected: We do not (and never will) log, track, or sell your browsing activity or personal information.
• Zero Telemetry: No Google Analytics or other trackers are embedded in the extension or website.
