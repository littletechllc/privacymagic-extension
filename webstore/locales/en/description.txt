The Web is a wonderful thing: it gives you access to the world's information. It's an essential part of modern life. Unfortunately, the Web is also a vehicle for mass surveillance. Everything you do online is being collected, stored, and analyzed by corporations and governments.

Privacy Magic provides thorough, layered protections to shield you from surveillance.

- Blocks ads, trackers, and annoyances
